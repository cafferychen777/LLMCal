LLMCal Use Cases

Quick meeting creation from highlighted text

Select: “Team sync tomorrow 2–3pm, Zoom https://zoom.us/j/123, remind me 10 minutes before” → Event with title, time, Zoom link, and reminder.

Recurring events from natural language

Select: “Weekly standup every Mon 9:30am, 30 minutes” → RRULE and duration set.

Parse attendees and locations

Select: “Product demo next Tue 3pm at HQ Boardroom with alice@example.com bob@example.com” → Location and attendees parsed.

Time zones and all‑day events

Select: “1:1 Thursday 10am PST (my time 1pm EST), 30 minutes” / “All-day offsite next Friday”.

Personal productivity

Select: “Pay rent on the 1st of every month, remind me 1 day before”.
